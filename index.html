About us


Our mission is to make healthy meals more convenient and accessible to the home cook, while allowing them to use any leftover ingredients from other cooking sessions. We believe this will give people easier access to educational resources for cooking, while also avoiding the stress of having to purchase new, single-use ingredients for every meal.
