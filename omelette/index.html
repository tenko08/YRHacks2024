Omlette (easy)

You selected omelettes!

Step 1


Crack the eggs into a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 2


Beat the eggs with a fork or whisk until the yolks and whites are well combined and slightly frothy.

Step 3


Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add a small pat of butter or a drizzle of oil and swirl it around to coat the bottom of the skillet.

Step 4


Once the butter is melted and the skillet is hot, pour the beaten eggs into the skillet.

Step 5


Let the eggs cook undisturbed for a few seconds until the edges start to set.

Step 6


Using a spatula, gently lift the edges of the omelette and tilt the skillet to allow the uncooked eggs to flow underneath.

Step 7


Continue cooking the omelette until the bottom is set but the top is still slightly runny.

Optional


If adding any fillings, sprinkle them evenly over one half of the omelette.

Step 8


Using the spatula, carefully fold the unfilled half of the omelette over the filling to form a half-moon shape.

Step 9


Cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until the omelette is cooked through and any cheese is melted.

Finished!


Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve.
